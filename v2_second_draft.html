Risk Factors

Lynch Syndrome

Genetic. MLH1/MSH2/MSH6/PMS2 mutations. 40–80% lifetime risk. ~3% of all CRC.

FAP (Familial Adenomatous Polyposis)

APC gene. Near 100% risk without colectomy. Hundreds of polyps develop.

Inflammatory Bowel Disease

Ulcerative colitis & Crohn's. Risk 2–8× depending on extent + duration.

Obesity & Inactivity

BMI ≥30 = RR ~1.3–1.5. Independent risk from sedentary lifestyle too.

Red & Processed Meat

IARC Group 1 carcinogen (processed meat). +18% risk per 50g/day.

Alcohol & Tobacco

Heavy drinking RR ~1.5. Smoking risk appears after 30–40 year latency.

Type 2 Diabetes

Hyperinsulinemia promotes adenoma growth. RR ~1.3–1.5.

Gut Microbiome

F. nucleatum enriched in CRC tumors. Emerging research area.

Western Diet Pattern

Processed foods, low fiber, high fat. Hypothesized driver of early-onset rise.
